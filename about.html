    About Full Swag Clothing

    Welcome to Full Swag Clothing, where fashion meets cultural diversity, innovation, and care! Established in 2015, Full Swag Clothing aims to provide a unique shopping experience that transcends ordinary fashion by embracing and celebrating South Africa's 11 vibrant cultures. We blend tradition with contemporary style, creating clothing that allows our customers to proudly express their identity through what they wear.

    Our Founders:

    Full Swag Clothing was brought to life by Shichabo Nkuna and a dynamic team that includes Nobuhle, Londeka, Bahati, Lungisani, and Nomfundo, alongside famous South African artist Kid Krestel and cultural ambassador Kwazikwenkosi. Together, this diverse group of visionaries has built a brand rooted in cultural pride, creativity, and customer care.

    Cultural Embracement:

    At Full Swag, we celebrate the unique beauty of South Africa’s 11 official cultures. Our collections incorporate traditional elements from each culture, allowing our customers to honor their heritage while enjoying modern, stylish designs. Whether you're Tsonga, Xhosa, Zulu, or from any of the other rich cultures, you’ll find clothing that speaks to your heart and heritage.

    Counselling Services:

    Fashion meets wellness at Full Swag. We’ve partnered with qualified medical professionals to offer counselling services to our customers. We believe in nurturing the mind, body, and soul. Whether you need support or simply advice, our professional counselling service is here to ensure that our customers feel their best both emotionally and physically.

    Colour Correction:

    Choosing the right colors for your skin tone has never been easier with our Colour Correction Service. Simply upload your picture directly on our website or send it via email, and our fashion experts will help you select outfits that perfectly complement your skin tone. Urgent requests can be processed instantly through our website, while non-urgent requests can be handled through email.

    Clothing Rentals:

    Looking for something stunning for a special occasion without the commitment of purchasing? Our Clothing Rental Service allows you to rent high-quality, stylish outfits at an affordable rate for a set period. Stay fashionable while keeping it budget-friendly!

    Buy Now, Pay Later:

    With our Credit Service, fashion becomes more accessible. You can now take home the clothing you love today and pay for it later, giving you the flexibility to manage your budget while staying stylish.

    Social Media Advertisement:

    We believe in reaching our customers where they are! Full Swag Clothing will advertise our products on popular platforms such as TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest. This ensures that those who do not have our app can still discover and engage with our latest collections. Stay tuned for creative and vibrant advertisements that showcase the essence of our brand!

    Our Vision:

    At Full Swag Clothing, we’re more than just a brand—we’re a movement that promotes cultural pride, style, and holistic well-being. Our founders are committed to making fashion not just an expression of self, but a celebration of heritage and a source of care for our community. Join us in this exciting journey where fashion, culture, and care come together!
